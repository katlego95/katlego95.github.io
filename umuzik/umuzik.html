The Umuzik show podcast, a place where people with opinions, be it Umuzi recruits or external collaborators can meet and engage in topics ranging from creativity to social injustices and personal topics. The whole aim of the podcast is to exchange information, come to possible solutions and most importantly the podcast becomes a place to learn and expand one’s perspective and gain insight and ideas.
